About ProLiftingClub

ProLiftingClub is a fitness content platform created by Gilberto Muniz, dedicated to sharing authentic strength training content from Lifting Club gym. Our mission is to document real training sessions — from heavy compound lifts to mobility work — and share them with athletes looking to improve their performance.

Our Content

We publish content across Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook, covering exercise demonstrations, workout tips, and training insights. Every video is filmed during actual training sessions to keep things real — no staged content, no scripted routines, just real lifting.

What We Cover

• Strength training (compound lifts, accessory work, progressive overload)

• Mobility and warm-up routines (shoulder mobility, hip mobility, recovery drills)

• Powerlifting (squat, bench press, deadlift technique and programming)

• Muscle group isolation (chest, back, shoulders, legs, arms)
