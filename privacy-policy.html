Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

1. Overview

ProLiftingClub is a fitness content platform that publishes workout videos and training content on our social media accounts. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data in connection with our website and social media integrations.

2. Data We Collect

ProLiftingClub does not collect, store, or share personal data from visitors of our website or viewers of our social media content. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics tools on this website. No personal information is requested or stored.

3. Social Media API Usage

ProLiftingClub uses the following third-party APIs to publish content exclusively on our own social media accounts:

• TikTok Content Posting API — Used to publish workout videos to the ProLiftingClub TikTok account.

• Instagram Graph API — Used to publish workout videos to the ProLiftingClub Instagram account.

• Facebook Graph API — Used to publish workout videos to the ProLiftingClub Facebook Page.

4. Data Access Scope

Our application (ProLiftingClub) accesses only the authenticated account owner's data. We do not access, collect, or process data from other platform users. No third-party user data is accessed through our API integrations. The APIs are used solely to publish content on our own accounts.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or distribute any data to third parties. No data is transmitted to external services beyond the three platforms listed above for content publishing purposes.

6. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data from users, there is no user data to retain or delete. Access tokens used for API integrations are stored securely and used only for content publishing.

7. Children's Privacy

Our content is intended for general audiences interested in fitness and strength training. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13, as we do not collect data from any users.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of our content after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy inquiries or questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: contact@proliftingclub.com
