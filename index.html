About ProLiftingClub

ProLiftingClub is a fitness content platform created by Gilberto Muniz, dedicated to sharing authentic strength training content from Lifting Club gym. Our mission is to document real training sessions — from heavy compound lifts to mobility work — and share them with athletes looking to improve their performance.

We publish content across Instagram, TikTok, and Facebook, covering exercise demonstrations, workout tips, and training insights. Every video is filmed during actual training sessions to keep things real.
